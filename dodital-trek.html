Dodital Trek

Dodital Trek is located in the Uttarkashi district of the Garhwal region in the state of Uttarakhand.
Dodital Lake is a 16km trek from Agora Village.
This freshwater lake is situated at a height of 3024 meters above sea level.

Based on mythology, Dodital is known as the birthplace of Lord Ganesha, hence the name ‘'Dhundi Tal'', ''Dundi'' meaning lord Ganesha and ''Tal'' lake, which means lake of lord Ganesha (गणेश का ताल). According to ''Shivpuran,'' lord Ganesh is born here, and lost his head. To reach Dodital, one should reach Uttarkashi / Agora by road and then trek to Dodital, A short jeep ride is available to take you to Agora.

Dodital Trek Highlights: - Dodital trek is one of the most sought treks in the world towering at a height of 3024 m and is known as the most beautiful lake and Birth place of lord Ganesha. - This trek will take you through some unknown parts of the region which are less discovered by people. - During your trek catch the glimpse of the mesmerizing views of Mt.Bandarpoonch(6,312m), Mt.Black peak(6,387), and the Greater Himalayan Range. -The fauna includes Monal, Grey, Shrike, Bush Robin, Pink Finch, Munia, Common Chiffchaff, Himalayan Whistling Thrush, Himalayan Tree Pie, swamp deer, musk deer, black bear, leopards, etc.

Itinerary

Day 1 : Uttarkashi/Dehradun/Rishikesh to Agora

Uttarkashi to Agora Distance: 28km by Car
Arrive in the wee hours at Dehradun / Rishikesh/Haridwar then drive to Uttarkashi, Frequent buses and shared taxis are available from Dehradun /Rishikesh bus stand to Uttarkashi After lunch drive to Agora the starting point of the trek. Agora is a quaint village with a population of 450. Agoda Village is an interesting spot for Village Tourism as you’ll get to see the rural lifestyle, taste the traditional cuisines, and witness the culture and traditions of the people. Most of the Pahadis as we refer to us are certified in mountaineering courses by NIM (Nehru Institute of Mountaineering).

Day 2 : Agora to Dodital

Trek: 16km.

After breakfast, follow the trail as it ascends through the forest to arrive at Majhi. From here, through rhododendron, Oak, Pine, and Bamboo Forests around the hillside to arrive at the beautiful lake of Dodital to Darwa Pass to Dodital.


Day 3 : Dodital to Darwa Pass to Dodital
  • Dodital to Darwa Trek: 5km one way
  • Darwa to Dodital Trek: 5km one way

After breakfast enjoys the beauty and serenity of the lake. For the one seeking a physical challenge, a climb atop the Darwa pass is very rewarding. You can see the magnificent Peaks of Bandar Poonch and Swargarohini evening back to camp.


Day 4 : Dodital to Agoda Village
  • Trek: 16km
  • Local Festival: June
  • Last day Stay at Agoda Village with local food

After breakfast return trek to the village of Agoda. Check in the local village house. Evening understands the life of local people and enjoys local food.
